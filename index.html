About the Roadshow

The Ohio River Valley of Appalachia — coal country — has long been the heart of the nation's industrial base. The Appalachian Manufacturing Action Plan (A-MAP) is a draft, multi-state roadmap for transforming that legacy into a hub for sustainable, equitable manufacturing. The Roadshow Series, hosted by ReImagine Appalachia in partnership with the American Manufacturing Communities Collaborative (AMCC), brings the plan to local communities to gather feedback and build buy-in before it's finalized.
